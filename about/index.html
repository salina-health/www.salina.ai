Salina Health Ltd

Salina Health is an early-stage technology company targeting psychological therapy.

We extract insights from language and voice to:

  • Saves therapists valuable time;
  • deliver better therapy; and
  • helps more patients access therapy.

How we work

We’re creating AI tools for therapy session analytics and ongoing patient monitoring. This could provide significant efficiencies at clinics with the growth of remote therapy providers, and add insights with language and voice metrics.

These services is provided as a software-as-a-service.

We prefer to partner with patient management software companies to deliver the technologies. If you’re a PMS interested in Salina, do get in touch.

Applications outside of mental health

The key technology is also applicable in physical health, and we are running a pilot project in oncology with an NHS partner.
